Why Cybersecurity Matters

Healthcare organizations manage sensitive patient data. Cyber threats like ransomware and data breaches pose serious risks to privacy and operations.

Best Practices

  • Data Encryption: Encrypt patient data at rest and in transit.
  • Access Control: Use multi-factor authentication and role-based access.
  • Regular Audits: Conduct periodic security assessments and risk evaluations.
  • Employee Training: Educate staff on phishing, password hygiene, and incident response.
  • Patch Management: Keep all software up-to-date with the latest security patches.
